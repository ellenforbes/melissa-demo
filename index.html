01 · About

Hey there 👋🏻

I'm Melissa — an artist who can't sit still. I paint walls, signs, props, costumes, and anything else that needs a little more oomph.

Faux finishes that fool the eye. Hand-lettered signs. Murals that turn a kid's bedroom into a portal. Costumes for the stage. If it can be painted, I'll probably try to paint it.

When I'm not painting, I'm teaching — both fine-art technique and the everyday handywoman stuff most people never got shown. (You really can patch that hole yourself.)

04 · FAQ & Inquiries

Got questions?

What types of projects do you take on?

Faux finishes, murals, hand-lettered signs, full-home interiors, props, costumes, and interactive event installs. If you're not sure — ask.

How do I book you?

Send me a note using the form on the right and pick the closest match from the dropdown. I'll reply within a few days with next steps and a quote.

Do you travel for jobs?

Yes — local jobs are easiest, but I travel for the right project. Travel outside my home area is added to the quote.

How does pricing work?

Every project is different, so I quote per job after we chat about scope, surface, materials, and timeline. Workshops and events have set rates that scale with group size.

What about deposits and timelines?

Most jobs need a deposit to lock in the date. Lead time varies by season — the sooner you reach out, the better.

Do you teach private workshops?

Yes — for individuals, small groups, schools, and community programs. Pick "Teaching" in the form and tell me what you'd like to learn.
